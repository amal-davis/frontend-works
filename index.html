Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating in South Asia. It is made with Indian spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish), or in some cases without any meat, using Paneer or simply vegetables. Occasionally, eggs and potatoes are also included.
